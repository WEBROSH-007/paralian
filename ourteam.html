Jesse Waits
Founder
Drawing upon more than 21 years of seasoned expertise within the hospitality domain of esteemed high-profile establishments, Jesse Waits, renowned owner and founder, has solidified his position as a prominent figure in the realms of Las Vegas and Los Angeles entertainment and hospitality industries. His career stands out for his commitment to curate unique guest experiences that blend operational excellence with creative strategy and business savvy, thereby redefining hospitality standards

His roles have included, Managing Partner at Wynn Resort Nightlife, where he worked closely with Steve Wynn for a decade. He further honed his skills as the Chief Experience Officer and Partner at Alon resorts and as the Chief Experience Officer & SVP at Red Rock Resorts, where he oversaw 29 casinos alongside Lorenzo Fertitta. Additionally, Jesse has lent his expertise to consulting for the 2022 World Cup in Qatar.
Gabriel Waits
Chief Operation Officer (COO)
As the partner and wellness architect of the 11•11 brand, Gabriel Waits has ventured into real estate development with a zeal for transforming spaces and creating impactful communities. At the helm of the wellness aspect of 11•11, Gabriel draws on his extensive knowledge, including a Bachelor's degree in Kinesiology and NASM certifications in Personal Training and Nutrition Coaching.

His holistic approach and deep understanding of physical health span an array of disciplines, from anatomy and physiology to biomechanics, nutrition, exercise and program design, prevention and care of sports injuries, psychology of sports and fitness, fitness and wellness strategies, as well as exercise physiology. Additionally, Gabriel's diverse experience in marketing, firefighting, and first-aid instruction endows him with a multifaceted skill set.

As the Administrative & Office Coordinator at Paralian, Marta manages essential day-to-day operations to ensure smooth and efficient office workflows. In-House Architect – Paralian
